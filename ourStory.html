our story

Lamps is a Vancouver based furniture company that caters to interior designers by showcasing their work in our store. Our most frequently featured item is, well you might have guessed but, lamps! We believe that lighting is the crucial accent piece to any home, and should have both quality and aesthetic.

our values

We value local interior designers in Vancouver, as we understand that it is hard to get known or noticed without a special network. We hope that by caring for the new emerging interior designers, we will be able to move towards a more sustainable city where resources can be easily reused.

return policy

All purchased items must be in their original, unopened packaging with proof of receipt within 64 days to return purchase. LAMPS is unable to take back the product if it does not meet the above requirements. Exchanges must meet the same requirements and can only be traded for other available products of equal or lesser value.
